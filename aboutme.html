I'm passionate about data analysis and machine learning.

With a PhD in Electrical Engineering and a Master's in Physics, I bring quantitative research skills and practical industry experience as an R&D engineer within the Australian automation industry.

My professional journey began in Saudi Arabia, where I pursued a Master's degree in Physics. During this time, I also took on the role of a lecturer at a dentistry college and initiated an educational program, introducing children to innovation skills through engaging science projects. This path later led me to earn two scholarships for my PhD at UWA, a significant and thrilling move in my career. These experiences showcase my self-motivation and unwavering commitment to learning and innovation.

Throughout my journey, I've exhibited the ability to quickly adapt and collaborate with individuals from diverse disciplines. My current dedication lies in applying data science to address real-world challenges, and continually enhancing my coding proficiency in C++, Matlab, R, and Python.
