I hold a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Western Australia and a Master's in Physics.

My academic work includes extensive data analysis, resulting in five scientific publications.

Additionally, I have three years of combined experience as a Data Scientist and Software Engineer.

I am proficient in Python, SQL, R, Matlab, and C++, with a strong foundation in advanced statistical modeling and machine learning techniques to solve complex problems.

Some of the key projects I've worked on include:

  • Developing advanced algorithms using Recursive Bayesian Classification (RBC) for time-series analysis of satellite images.
  • Creating an image-quality detection tool to predict satellite image quality.
  • Building a sun-position tool by analysing metadata and satellite imagery.
  • Building regression models and conducting feature selection using Lasso and GAN to evaluate factors influencing AI models.
  • Designing a data catalog of over 200,000 images by engineering features to assign locations and reference the corresponding Terrestrial Ecoregions of the World (TEOW).
  • Building user interface using AutoCAD to automate electrical drawing generation.
  • Developing models of optical signals in Matlab to perform quantitative analysis of the performance of optical materials.
